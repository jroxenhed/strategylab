Half-spread formula: `(ask ŌłÆ bid) / (2 ├Ś mid) ├Ś 10,000` bps per leg. Conservative floor: `max(2.0, half_spread_bps)`. Source label: `"spread-derived"` shown as "live spread" in StrategyBuilder.
